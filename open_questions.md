# Open Questions

Use this file as the durable backlog for research, data, identification, and workflow questions.

## Identification

- [ ] What is the main empirical strategy?
- [ ] What assumptions are required for causal interpretation?
- [ ] What pre-trends, placebo tests, or balance checks are needed?
- [ ] What are the main exclusion-restriction or omitted-variable concerns?

## Measurement

- [ ] What are the main outcomes?
- [ ] What are the treatment or exposure variables?
- [ ] What units, currencies, deflators, or price bases are used?
- [ ] Are key variables comparable across time, geography, or data sources?

## Data Structure

- [ ] What is the unit of observation?
- [ ] What are the key identifiers?
- [ ] Are there many-to-one, one-to-many, or panel linkage issues?
- [ ] Are there geographic or administrative boundary changes to handle?

## Sample and Specification

- [ ] What are the main sample restrictions?
- [ ] What controls, fixed effects, and clustering are planned?
- [ ] Should specifications be registered in `code/empirical_analysis/spec_registry.md`?

## Writing and Framing

- [ ] What should the paper claim?
- [ ] What should the paper not claim?
- [ ] What are the main contributions?

## Workflow

- [ ] Have real raw data been added or linked?
- [ ] Has `examples/data_documentation/` been deleted or explicitly retained with approval?
- [ ] Are interpreter paths recorded in `config/paths.yaml`?
- [ ] Are external dependencies documented?
